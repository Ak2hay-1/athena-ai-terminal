# Athena AI Terminal

## Project Overview

**Version:** 1.0 (Planning)
**Project Owner:** Akshay Patil

---

# 1. Vision

Athena AI Terminal is a desktop/web-based AI trading platform designed to monitor financial markets continuously, analyze market conditions using technical analysis, market structure, and news, and provide intelligent trading recommendations.

The system will initially focus on **XAUUSD (Gold)** but will be designed from the beginning to support additional symbols such as:

* XAUUSD
* EURUSD
* GBPUSD
* USDJPY
* BTCUSD
* ETHUSD
* NAS100
* US30

The platform will emphasize transparency, explainability, and risk management over fully autonomous trading.

# 7. Development Strategy

The project will be built incrementally.

Each module must:

* Compile successfully.
* Be tested.
* Be committed to Git.
* Be documented.

Only after a module is complete will development continue to the next module.
